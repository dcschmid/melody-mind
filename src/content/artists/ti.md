biography: Clifford Joseph Harris Jr., known professionally as T.I. and Tip, born on September 25, 1980, in Atlanta, Georgia, is an American rapper, songwriter, and actor who rose to prominence in the early 2000s as one of the leading figures in Southern hip-hop, known for his smooth flow, entrepreneurial ventures, and influence on hip-hop culture. He achieved breakthrough success with his 2003 album Trap Muzik, which established his signature trap sound, and subsequent albums like King (2006) and Paper Trail (2008) achieved massive commercial success, with Paper Trail becoming one of the best-selling rap albums of the decade. T.I. has sold over 24 million records worldwide and has successfully expanded his career into acting, television production, and business ventures. His influence on Southern hip-hop and trap music has been significant.

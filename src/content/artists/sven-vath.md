He began his DJ career in the early 1980s and rose to prominence in the 1990s with releases on labels like Harthouse and Cocoon Recordings, establishing himself as a pioneer of the trance and techno scenes. His annual Sound of the Season sets and his work with Cocoon Records have made him a fixture at clubs and festivals worldwide. Veth's influence on electronic music culture and his role in establishing Frankfurt as a center for techno music have been widely recognized.

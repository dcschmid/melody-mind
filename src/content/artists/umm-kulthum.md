biography: Umm Kulthum was an Egyptian singer and actress who became one of the most influential and beloved voices in Arabic music history. Born in the Nile Delta, she began performing as a child and developed her powerful, emotive vocal style through years of training and performance. Her repertoire included classical Arabic music, religious songs, and modern compositions, all delivered with her distinctive vocal technique and dramatic phrasing. She became a cultural icon across the Arab world, transcending national and generational boundaries. Her influence on Arabic music and her status as a symbol of Egyptian and Arab identity remain unparalleled.

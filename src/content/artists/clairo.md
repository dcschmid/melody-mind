She began posting music to YouTube as a teenager and gained attention with her 2017 EP Pretty Girl, which featured the viral hit Pretty Girl. Her subsequent albums including Immunity (2019) and Sling (2021) have showcased her growth as a sophisticated songwriter, earning critical acclaim and positioning her as an important voice in contemporary indie music. Cottrill has been praised for her ability to create intimate, emotionally resonant music that defies easy categorization.

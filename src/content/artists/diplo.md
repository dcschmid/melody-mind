biography: Thomas Wesley Pentz, known professionally as Diplo, born on November 10, 1978, in Tupelo, Mississippi, is an American DJ, record producer, songwriter, and founder of the Mad Decent record label, widely regarded as one of the most influential and versatile electronic music producers of his generation, known for his genre-blending approach and ability to work across multiple styles. He rose to prominence in the 2000s with his blend of electronic, hip-hop, and world music influences, achieving breakthrough success with the Major Lazer project (2008) and the Jack Ü collaboration (2015). Diplo has worked with artists across multiple genres, producing hits for Beyonce, Madonna, and many others. His influence on electronic music and his role in bringing global sounds to mainstream audiences have made him one of the most successful and respected producers in the industry.

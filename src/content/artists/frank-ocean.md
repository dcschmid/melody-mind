His follow-up album Blonde (2016) and visual album Endless (2016) cemented his status as a visionary artist, with Blonde becoming one of the most acclaimed albums of the decade. Ocean's influence on R&B and his willingness to address personal topics including his sexuality have made him a significant figure in popular music.

She rose to prominence in the 2010s with her debut album LP1 (2014), which was critically acclaimed for its innovative sound and emotional depth. Her subsequent work including the album Magdalene (2019) has established her as one of the most innovative and critically respected artists in contemporary music. FKA twigs has been praised for her artistic vision, vocal abilities, and willingness to push boundaries in both music and visual art.

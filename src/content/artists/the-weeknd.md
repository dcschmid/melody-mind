biography: Abel Tesfaye, born on February 16, 1990, in Toronto, Canada, known professionally as The Weeknd, is a Canadian singer, songwriter, and producer who has become one of the most successful and influential artists of his generation, known for his falsetto vocals, dark themes of drug use and hedonism, and evolution from SoundCloud artist to global superstar. He rose to prominence in the early 2010s with a series of mixtapes that established his unique sound blending R&B, pop, and electronic influences. His albums including Beauty Behind the Madness (2015), Starboy (2016), and After Hours (2020) have achieved massive commercial success, with multiple Grammy Awards recognizing his contributions. The Weeknd has sold over 75 million records worldwide and has influenced contemporary R&B with his artistic vision.

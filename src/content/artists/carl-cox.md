biography: Carl Cox, born on July 29, 1962, in Oldham, England, is a British DJ, record producer, and remixer widely regarded as one of the most influential and enduring figures in electronic dance music, known for his technical proficiency, eclectic taste, and ability to read and energize crowds across multiple genres of dance music. He began his DJ career in the early 1980s and became a pioneer of the UK rave scene in the late 1980s and 1990s, releasing influential tracks and albums that helped define the sound of techno and house music. Cox has headlined major festivals and clubs worldwide for over four decades and has remained relevant through his adaptability to changing trends in electronic music. His mixes, compilations, and radio shows have influenced generations of DJs and electronic music fans worldwide.

Her subsequent albums, including Da Real World (1999), Miss E... So Addictive (2001), and Under Construction (2002), have been praised for their creativity and innovation, with Under Construction becoming one of the best-selling rap albums of all time. Elliott has won multiple Grammy Awards and has influenced generations of female artists who have followed in her footsteps.

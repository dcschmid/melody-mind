biography: Sven Veth, born on March 26, 1964, in Oberursel, West Germany, is a German DJ and electronic music producer widely regarded as one of the most influential and enduring figures in techno and trance music, known for his DJ sets at Cocoon Club in Frankfurt and his role in shaping the sound of German electronic music. He began his DJ career in the early 1980s and rose to prominence in the 1990s with releases on labels like Harthouse and Cocoon Recordings, establishing himself as a pioneer of the trance and techno scenes. His annual Sound of the Season sets and his work with Cocoon Records have made him a fixture at clubs and festivals worldwide. Veth's influence on electronic music culture and his role in establishing Frankfurt as a center for techno music have been widely recognized.

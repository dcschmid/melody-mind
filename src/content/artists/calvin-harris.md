biography: Adam Wiles, known professionally as Calvin Harris, born on January 17, 1984, in Dumfries, Scotland, is a Scottish DJ, record producer, and singer-songwriter who has become one of the most successful and influential electronic music producers of his generation, known for his chart-topping dance-pop hits and ability to work with top artists across multiple genres. He rose to prominence in the late 2000s with his blend of disco-influenced electronic dance music and achieved massive commercial success with albums like 18 Months (2012), which produced five UK number one singles. Harris has sold over 50 million records worldwide and has collaborated with artists including Rihanna, Frank Ocean, and Dua Lipa. His influence on electronic dance music and pop music has made him one of the most successful British artists of all time.

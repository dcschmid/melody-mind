She began her career as an actress on Broadway and Nickelodeon before launching a music career that achieved massive commercial success with albums like My Everything (2014), Dangerous Woman (2016), and Thank U, Next (2019). Her influence on pop music and her advocacy for various social causes have made her a significant cultural figure.

biography: James Blake Litherland, born on September 26, 1988, in London, England, is an English singer, songwriter, producer, and multi-instrumentalist widely regarded as one of the most innovative and influential artists in contemporary electronic music and alternative R&B, known for his atmospheric productions, emotional vocals, and genre-blending approach. He began his career in the early 2010s with self-released EPs and achieved breakthrough success with his self-titled debut album (2011), which was critically acclaimed for its innovative sound. His subsequent work including Overgrown (2013) and Assume Form (2019) has established him as one of the most respected artists in contemporary music, with multiple Grammy Awards recognizing his contributions. Blake has also become a sought-after producer for other artists.

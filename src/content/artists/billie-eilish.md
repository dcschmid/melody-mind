She achieved breakthrough success with the single Ocean Eyes (2015), which went viral on SoundCloud and established her as a rising star. Her 2019 debut album When We All Fall Asleep, Where Do We Go? debuted at number one on the Billboard 200 and became one of the best-selling albums of the year, featuring hits including Bad Guy, which won Record of the Year at the Grammys. Eilish has sold over 100 million records worldwide and has been praised for her artistic maturity, fashion sense, and advocacy for mental health awareness.

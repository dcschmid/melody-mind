biography: Shaffer Chimere Smith, known professionally as Ne-Yo, born on October 18, 1979, in Little Rock, Arkansas, is an American singer, songwriter, producer, and actor known for his smooth R&B vocals, songwriting skills, and contributions to the neo-soul and contemporary R&B genres. He began his career as a songwriter for other artists before launching his solo career in 2005 with his debut album In My Own Words, which established his signature sound blending R&B, soul, and pop elements. His subsequent albums, including Because of You (2007), Year of the Gentleman (2008), and Libra Scale (2010), have demonstrated his growth as an artist and songwriter. Ne-Yo has won multiple Grammy Awards and has written hits for numerous other artists while maintaining a successful solo career spanning over two decades.

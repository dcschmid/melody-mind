biography: Jeffrey Mills, known professionally as Jeff Mills, born on June 18, 1963, in Detroit, Michigan, is an American DJ, record producer, and composer widely regarded as one of the most technically proficient and influential techno DJs and producers in electronic music history. He rose to prominence in the early 1990s as part of the Detroit techno scene and later as a solo artist, releasing influential tracks and albums that helped define the sound of techno music. Mills has been known for his marathon DJ sets, innovative mixing techniques, and ability to manipulate crowds through his precise and energetic performances. His work with his record labels Axis Records and his film scores and soundtracks have expanded his influence beyond the club scene into broader artistic and cinematic contexts.

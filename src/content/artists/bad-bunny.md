biography: Benito Antonio Martínez Ocasio, known professionally as Bad Bunny, born on March 10, 1994, in San Juan, Puerto Rico, is a Puerto Rican rapper, singer, and producer who has become one of the most influential and successful Latin artists of his generation, known for his distinctive voice, genre-blending approach to Latin trap and reggaeton, and his unapologetic embrace of his Puerto Rican identity. He rose to prominence in 2017 with his debut album X 100pre and subsequent releases including the massively successful YHLQMDLG (2020) and El Último Tour Del Mundo (2020), which made him the first Latin artist to top the Billboard 200 with a Spanish-language album. Bad Bunny has sold over 80 million records worldwide and has influenced the evolution of Latin trap, while his willingness to address social issues and embrace his eccentric style has made him a cultural icon.

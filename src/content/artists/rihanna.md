biography: Robyn Rihanna Fenty, born on February 20, 1988, in Saint Michael, Barbados, is a Barbadian singer, songwriter, businesswoman, and actress who has become one of the most successful and influential artists of her generation, selling over 250 million records worldwide. She began her career in the mid-2000s with her debut album Music of the Sun and achieved breakthrough success with A Girl Like Me (2006) and the single Umbrella (2007). Her subsequent albums including Good Girl Gone Bad (2007), Rated R (2009), Loud (2010), and Anti (2016) have showcased her artistic evolution and vocal prowess. Rihanna has won 9 Grammy Awards and has expanded her influence beyond music into fashion and beauty with her Fenty brand, making her a significant cultural and business figure.

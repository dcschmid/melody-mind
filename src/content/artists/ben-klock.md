biography: Ben Klock, born on March 14, 1976, in Eisenach, East Germany, is a German DJ and techno producer widely regarded as one of the most technically proficient and influential techno artists of his generation, known for his marathon DJ sets, precise mixing skills, and association with the Berlin techno scene. He began his DJ career in the 1990s and rose to prominence in the 2000s as a resident DJ at the legendary Berghain club in Berlin, where his sets have become legendary among techno enthusiasts. Klock has released acclaimed music on labels like Ostgut Ton and his own Klockworks label, influencing generations of techno producers and DJs. His reputation as one of techno music's most important figures has made him a fixture at festivals and clubs worldwide.

biography: Sir Robert Bryson Hall II, born on January 22, 1990, in Gaithersburg, Maryland, known professionally as Logic, is an American rapper, songwriter, and producer known for his technical proficiency, conscious lyrics, and ability to address social issues while maintaining commercial success. He rose to prominence in the 2010s with a series of mixtapes and albums including The Incredible True Story (2015), which told a science fiction narrative, and Everybody (2017), which addressed themes of mortality and identity. His 2019 album Confessions of a Dangerous Mind was commercially successful, and his final album No Pressure (2020) concluded his career with a reflection on fatherhood. Logic has been praised for his technical skills and his willingness to address issues including mental health, racism, and social justice.

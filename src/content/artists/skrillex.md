biography: Sonny John Moore, known professionally as Skrillex, born on January 15, 1988, in Los Angeles, California, is an American DJ, record producer, and songwriter who rose to prominence in the early 2010s as one of the defining figures of the EDM explosion, known for his aggressive, bass-heavy sound and high-energy live performances. He began his career as the lead singer of the post-hardcore band From First to Last before launching his solo career as Skrillex and achieving breakthrough success with the EP Scary Monsters and Nice Sprites (2010). His subsequent work including the album Recess (2014) and collaborations with Diplo as Jack Ü brought electronic music to mainstream audiences. Skrillex has won multiple Grammy Awards and has influenced the development of electronic dance music globally.

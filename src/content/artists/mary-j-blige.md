Her subsequent albums, including My Life (1994), No More Drama (2001), and The Breakthrough (2005), have showcased her artistic evolution and emotional depth as a songwriter. Blige has sold over 50 million records worldwide and has successfully transitioned into acting, earning Academy Award nominations for her roles in films like Mudbound (2017).

She achieved breakthrough success at age 16 with her debut single Royals (2013), which topped charts worldwide and established her as a voice of her generation. Her debut album Pure Heroine (2013) was critically acclaimed for its minimalist production and intelligent lyrics about fame, youth, and culture. Her follow-up album Melodrama (2017) was even more widely acclaimed, with many considering it one of the greatest albums of the decade. Lorde has been praised for her artistic vision and her ability to create music that transcends genre conventions.

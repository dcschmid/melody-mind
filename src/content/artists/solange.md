biography: Solange Piaget Knowles, born on June 24, 1986, in Houston, Texas, is an American singer, songwriter, and actress known for her innovative approach to R&B and soul, blending genres including jazz, funk, and electronic music into a distinctive artistic vision. She began her career in the early 2000s with her debut album Solo Star (2002) and achieved breakthrough success with her 2016 album A Seat at the Table, which was critically acclaimed for its sophisticated production and exploration of black identity and experiences. Her follow-up album When I Get Home (2019) continued her artistic evolution, cementing her status as one of the most innovative artists in contemporary R&B. Knowles has been praised for her willingness to experiment and her commitment to creating music that defies easy categorization.

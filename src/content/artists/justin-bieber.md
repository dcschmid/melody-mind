biography: Justin Drew Bieber, born on March 1, 1994, in Stratford, Ontario, Canada, is a Canadian singer, songwriter, and pop superstar who rose to prominence as a teenager through YouTube videos and became one of the most successful and influential pop artists of the 2010s and 2020s. He was discovered by talent manager Scooter Braun in 2008 and signed to RBMG Records, releasing his debut EP My World (2009) which made him the first solo artist to have all seven songs from an album chart on the Billboard Hot 100. His debut album My World 2.0 (2010) and subsequent albums have established him as a global pop phenomenon with massive commercial success and a devoted fan base known as Beliebers. Bieber has sold over 150 million records worldwide and has successfully transitioned from teen pop idol to mature pop and R&B artist through albums like Purpose (2015) and Justice (2021).

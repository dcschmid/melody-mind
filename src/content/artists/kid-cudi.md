biography: "Scott Mescudi, known professionally as Kid Cudi, born on January 30, 1984, in Cleveland, Ohio, is an American rapper, singer, songwriter, and producer who has become one of the most influential artists in alternative hip-hop and emo rap, known for his introspective lyrics about mental health, his nasal flow, and his influence on a generation of artists. He rose to prominence with his debut album Man on the Moon: The End of Day (2009), which established his signature blend of hip-hop, rock, and electronic influences. His subsequent work including Man on the Moon II: The Legend of Mr. Rager (2010) and his 2020 album Man on the Moon III: The Chosen continued his legacy as a pioneer of emo rap and mental health awareness in hip-hop. Cudi has influenced numerous artists and has been praised for his willingness to address difficult topics."

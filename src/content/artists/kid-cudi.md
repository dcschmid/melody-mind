He rose to prominence with his debut album Man on the Moon: The End of Day (2009), which established his signature blend of hip-hop, rock, and electronic influences. His subsequent work including Man on the Moon II: The Legend of Mr. Rager (2010) and his 2020 album Man on the Moon III: The Chosen continued his legacy as a pioneer of emo rap and mental health awareness in hip-hop. Cudi has influenced numerous artists and has been praised for his willingness to address difficult topics."

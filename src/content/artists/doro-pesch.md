biography: Doro Pesch is a German heavy metal singer who has been a pioneering figure in metal music for over four decades. Born in Dusseldorf, West Germany, she rose to fame as the lead vocalist of the band Warlock, which became one of the first successful German metal bands internationally. After Warlock disbanded, she embarked on a successful solo career, becoming one of the most prominent female metal vocalists in the world. Known for her powerful vocals and energetic live performances, she has maintained a dedicated global fanbase while bridging the gap between heavy metal and hard rock. Her longevity and consistent artistic output have made her an icon in the metal community.

The Latin collection covers several distinct musical lanes rather than treating the label
as one sound. Afro-Cuban jazz and salsa-derived percussion sit beside bolero, Latin pop,
rock, reggaeton, and regional acoustic influences. Brass, congas, nylon-string guitar, and
electronic bass appear in different combinations, depending on the place and conflict at
the center of an album.

Story gives those choices direction. The catalog moves through Havana nights, family
histories, migration, neighborhood change, romance, and political anger. A dance rhythm
may carry celebration in one song and defiance in the next. Quieter tracks often hold the
personal consequences that the larger choruses leave unresolved.

These albums are AI-assisted productions built around characters, settings, and connected
track sequences. They do not stand in for the full range of Latin music or a particular
regional tradition. Each is an imagined project with a defined musical palette, using
rhythm and melody to keep its story in motion.

The Latin shelf brings MelodyMind Music into songs shaped by streets, ports, balconies,
family rooms, memory, and rhythm that carries more than celebration. Movement matters
here, but so does the reason a person starts moving.

These records use bright color and momentum, but the strongest moments are human-sized: a
voice at a window, a last serenade, a procession, a harbor, a kitchen table, or a city
holding several histories at once. The rhythm opens the door; the story decides who walks
through it.

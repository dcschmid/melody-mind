## About This Album

The record opens with "Idiot in Charge" and barely lets up. Twelve tracks, twelve jabs at
the machinery of modern strongman politics — ego, loyalty oaths, television theatrics,
hollow nationalism. The album title track closes the record with a chant-along hook that
anyone who's ever held a handmade sign in a crowd will recognize.

The lyrics mock the spectacle throughout, but they never lose sight of the damage
underneath. "Orange Throne" and "Red Hat Parade" go for the obvious targets with a
sledgehammer; "Truth in a Body Bag" is the sharpest cut — the bassline alone justifies the
runtime. "Make America Shut Up" is the track that will get stuck in your head first; "Cult
of Volume" is the one that earns the most repeat listens.

Musically, the album skips polish entirely. Distorted guitars, pounding drums, shouted
hooks, gang vocals — it was recorded to sound like a basement show, and nothing on this
record pretends otherwise.

## Production Notes

All tracks were generated with AI music models, then processed for the final sound. No
human performance recordings are used.

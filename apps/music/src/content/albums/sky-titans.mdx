## About This Album

The album opens with "Rise Above the Earth" — a full orchestral build that sets the
register for everything that follows. Twenty tracks, twenty variations on the same
premise: camera goes up, world gets bigger. Track 9, "Beyond the Mountains", is the
longest piece and the most layered; track 6, "Into the Vast Unknown", is the shortest and
works as a palette cleanser between the heavier passages.

The sound is trailer-style orchestral throughout. Brass themes, string movements,
percussion that hits like a door slam, choir drops in on the bigger moments. It is music
made for the shot where the drone clears the treeline and the valley opens up underneath.

Tracks 2 through 8 form the most emotionally consistent stretch — the energy builds and
releases across those seven. "Storm Over the Horizon", "Skyfall Kingdom", "The Last
Ascent" — each one earns its place in that sequence. The back half spreads wider: "Dawn
Over the Valley", "Ocean of Giants", "Titans of the Wind", "Roar of the Horizon" to close.

The album is built for drone footage. It does not try to be subtle about it.

## Production Notes

All tracks were generated with AI music models, then processed for the final orchestral
sound. No human performance recordings are used.

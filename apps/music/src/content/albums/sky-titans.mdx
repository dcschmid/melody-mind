## About the Album

Sky Titans is an aviation score with more lift than grit. Where Afterburner Horizon feels
tactical, this album feels mythic: aircraft, mountains, weather, and altitude treated as a
test of nerve and scale.

The opening tracks are built around ascent. "Rise Above the Earth" and "Wings of Fire"
give the record a clear upward pull, while "Empire in the Clouds" turns height into power.
The best action writing comes when the album remembers that flight is not only speed. It
is exposure, weather, thin air, and the constant possibility of falling.

Brass and strings carry the heroic line, with percussion giving the music its engine. The
album's large gestures work because they are tied to physical images: summit, storm,
horizon, sky. Sky Titans is broad, cinematic, and cleanly aimed at the feeling of looking
down from dangerous altitude.

## Production Notes

All tracks were generated with AI music models, then processed for the final orchestral
sound. No human performance recordings are used.

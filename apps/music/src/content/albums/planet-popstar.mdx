## About the Album

Planet Popstar treats outer space as a pop stage, which is exactly the right level of
seriousness for the album. The record is bright, quick, and visual: lightsticks become
launch signals, planets become dance floors, and gravity mostly exists so the hooks can
beat it.

The K-pop structure gives the fantasy a strong frame. "라이트스틱 발사! (Launch the
Lightstick!)" and "팝스타 행성 (Popstar Planet)" open with full-performance energy, while
"금성의 디스코 (Disco on Venus)" and "화성이 부른다 (Mars Is Calling)" move the concept
through different colors instead of repeating one glitter effect.

The album is commercial by design, but it is more fun when it leans into craft: clean
transitions, tight choruses, bright vocal stacks, and arrangements that feel made for
choreography. Planet Popstar is not deep space. It is stage space, and it knows how to use
the lights.

## Production Notes

All tracks were generated with AI music models, then processed for the final sound. No

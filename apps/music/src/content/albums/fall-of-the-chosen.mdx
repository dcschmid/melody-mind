## About This Album

Fall of the Chosen spans 13 tracks across roughly 55 minutes of runtime. The album follows
one figure from early promise through collapse into something harder. Orchestral
arrangements, distorted guitars, and choir vocals carry the narrative.

The story isn't about war. It's about what happens when someone decides to outrun fate
instead of accepting it.

Track one introduces a child marked for something enormous. By track seven, the weight of
that designation starts cracking the structure. Track nine hits rivers of fire — the
turning point. By the time you reach track thirteen, the transformation is done, and
there's nothing left of the original name.

Musically, the album moves from cleaner production early on into something denser and more
unstable as the narrative progresses. Strings increase. Guitars tighten. The mix gets
claustrophobic. This wasn't accidental.

If you want something that follows a clear arc from hope to ruin, this does that. Thirteen
chapters, no interludes, no breathers.

## Production Notes

All tracks were generated with AI music models, then processed for the final sound. No
human performance recordings are used.

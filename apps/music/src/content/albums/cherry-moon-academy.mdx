## About the Album

Cherry Moon Academy works best when it treats magic as a school-day detail rather than a
grand mythology. The album moves through classrooms, rooftops, lockers, festivals, and
graduation rituals with the light touch of school pop: bright synths, clean guitars, quick
emotional turns, and choruses built for group voices. Its fantasy is soft, but the
feelings underneath are ordinary enough to land.

"벚꽃 종이비행기 (Cherry Paper Airplane)" opens with the kind of image the album keeps
returning to: small, handmade, easy to lose. "달빛 출석부 (Moonlight Attendance)" gives
the academy its central mood, half classroom and half dream. By "마법서 첫 페이지 (First
Page of the Spellbook)," the record has established its rules. Lessons are emotional
before they are magical.

The early run is all nervous movement. "심장 알림음 (Heartbeat Notification)" is the
first-love song in modern school uniform, where a phone alert and a heartbeat feel like
the same instrument. "옥상 별자리 (Rooftop Constellation)" widens the view without leaving
campus, and "비밀 사물함 (Secret Locker)" understands how teenage pop turns small private
spaces into whole worlds.

The album’s middle section is more playful, but not empty. "분홍빛 주문 (Pink Spell)"
leans into bubblegum color, while "체육관 유성우 (Gymnasium Meteor Shower)" gives the set
one of its best scenes: a school gym briefly becoming a sky. "고백 예보 (Confession
Forecast)" is the obvious confession track, but the title keeps it charmingly practical.
Feelings arrive like weather. Everyone can see the clouds forming before anyone says the
sentence.

"눈물 결정체 (Crystal Tears)" shifts the record into its second emotional register. The
production can stay bright, but the writing is no longer only about wonder. It is about
what students carry when the semester, the friendship, or the crush starts to change
shape. "졸업 전야제 (Graduation Eve)" catches that suspended night before an ending, when
a school feels too familiar and already half gone.

The final stretch gives the album its strongest sense of closure. "우리만의 교가 (Our Own
School Anthem)" is less an official song than a private pledge between friends. "문이
열리는 밤 (The Night the Gate Opens)" turns the academy from a place into a threshold.
Then "다시 피는 달 (Moon Blooming Again)" closes with an image that fits the album’s
scale: not a huge victory, just the return of light in a different form.

Cherry Moon Academy is sugary by design, but it is not only sugar. Its best moments come
from how specifically it understands school-pop memory: the rooftop after class, the
locker no one else opens, the song a group writes for itself, the strange sadness of
leaving a place that once made you braver.
